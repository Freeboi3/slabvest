How it works

Simple. Accurate.
Built for investors.

01

Add your slabs

Log your graded cards by player, set, variant, grade, and purchase price. Takes under 30 seconds per card.

02

Live market prices

We pull real eBay sold data daily. Your portfolio is always priced against actual market comps — not estimates.

03

Track P&L

See your cost basis, current value, and total return on every card. Know exactly what you're up or down.

04

Market intelligence

Follow trending players, top movers, and recent big sales. Stay ahead of the market — not behind it.

📊

Portfolio dashboard

Your total value, daily change, and 30-day performance at a glance. Like Robinhood, but for your slabs.

💰

Profit & Loss tracking

Cost basis, ROI, and total return per card and across your whole collection. Know your real numbers.

🔔

Price alerts

Set buy and sell targets. Get notified the moment a card hits your price — so you never miss the move.

📈

Price history charts

30, 90, and 365-day price charts for any graded card. Spot trends and time your entries with confidence.

🏆

Pop report data

PSA and BGS population report inline. Understand scarcity instantly — know if supply is growing or tight.

🤖

AI insights COMING SOON

Liquidity scores, sell timing recommendations, and market regime detection — powered by real transaction data.
